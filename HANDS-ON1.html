STAND ALONE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

In Taguig, SASHS (Stand Alone Senior High School) is the senior high school unit within Taguig Integrated School (TIS). Although located within the TIS complex, SASHS operates independently with its own administration and leadership, separate from the junior high and elementary levels.
